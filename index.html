What changed in v3.0

v1.0 was the monochrome chassis. v2 added an accent and depth — in a green that turned out to be too polite. v3.0 replaces it with Redline: blunt, classic, and ours. The grays and the depth system stay; the attitude changed:

  • Redline (#ff2b36) — the engineer's red pen on the spec, the engine at redline. Revolutionary and classic at once. See Colors.
  • The strike — the new signature gesture: a red line through the rejected. Ship data, not excuses. See Graphics.
  • Sharp edges — corner radius cut from 6px to 2px across the system. Blunt surfaces, Swiss discipline.
  • The one-weapon rule — unchanged: exactly one red statement per surface; glow, bridge tones, and fades are atmosphere and don't count.
  • Depth stays — bridge tones (Flare, Red 900, Ink Red) and the four gradient recipes, recast in red.
  • The spec voice & the pulse — JetBrains Mono labels and stats; the red pulse with fading tail on the infinity loop; the red 8 for avatars. See Fonts and Logos.
